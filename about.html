About Me

My Story

As a UX/UI designer, I bring a unique blend of creativity, empathy, and technical expertise to every project. My background in Technical Writing and Communication (Bachelor’s degree) and Interactive Media Systems with a focus on Mobile Experience (Master’s degree) has equipped me with the tools to craft user-centered, accessible designs that solve real-world problems. Over the past 5+ years, I’ve gained professional experience working in startups, agencies, and corporations, where I honed my ability to design intuitive and impactful user experiences.

In addition to my design expertise, I have acquired knowledge in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which enhances my ability to collaborate effectively with developers and understand the implementation process. My approach to design is rooted in a human-centered philosophy, leveraging tools like Figma, Sketch, and Zeplin, as well as techniques such as usability testing, prototyping, and accessibility-focused design. What sets me apart is my passion for inclusivity and accessibility. By combining my technical communication background, mobile-first design expertise, and front-end development knowledge, I am dedicated to creating seamless, meaningful user experiences that empower and connect people. I thrive in dynamic environments, working collaboratively with cross-functional teams to deliver solutions that truly make a difference.

Outside of work, I’m an optimistic and humorous person who loves staying active and adventurous. My personal interests include bouldering, inline skating, beach volleyball, snowboarding, hiking, and exploring new cultures through travel. I also enjoy spending time outdoors with my dog, who is my loyal companion on my adventures. These passions not only fuel my creativity but also inspire me to approach challenges with energy, positivity, and a sense of fun.
