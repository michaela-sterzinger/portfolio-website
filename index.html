Zebra

As the UX/UI designer for ZEBRA 2.0 and ZEBRA Online, I developed user journeys, concepts, and high-fidelity designs to modernize the grant application process for Bremen citizens, associations, and organizations. Leveraging tools such as Figma, Sketch, and Zeplin, and adhering to WCAG AA and AAA accessibility standards, I contributed to creating intuitive and universally accessible platforms that streamline application submissions and status tracking.

Energy App

As the UX/UI Designer and UX Researcher for the Energy App, I employed a human-centered design process to develop a modern, accessible application that addresses key user needs. The app allows users to visualize energy and water consumption, manage tariffs, record meter readings (with historical tracking), and access bills with ease. By conducting iterative usability tests and leveraging tools such as Figma, I created user flows, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs to deliver a seamless and inclusive user experience, adhering to accessibility standards.
